Privacy Policy

Flâneur · Effective date: 19 June 2026

Flâneur is a personal guide to offbeat, storied places, sorted by how far they are from where you’re standing. This policy explains what the app does — and mostly doesn’t do — with your information.

The short version: Flâneur has no accounts, no login, and no server of our own that collects your data. Everything you do in the app — saved walks, check-ins, visited places, achievements and preferences — is stored only on your own device. We do not see it, receive it, or sell it.

Who we are

Flâneur is an independent, single-author project. The app is a self-contained web app served as static files (currently via GitHub Pages). There is no Flâneur account system and no Flâneur application server that stores user data.

Information stored on your device

To make the app work, the following is saved locally in your browser/device storage (localStorage) and stays there:

  • Saved and recorded walks (including a recorded route, if you track one)
  • Check-ins, visited places, streaks and achievements
  • Preferences such as your selected city and recent searches
  • A short-lived local cache of nearby results and weather, to reduce repeat requests

This information never leaves your device through Flâneur. You can erase all of it at any time by clearing this site’s data in your browser settings (or deleting the app if you installed it to your home screen).

Location

If you grant permission, the app uses your device’s location to sort places by distance and to power the live, radar and walk-tracking features. Your location is processed on your device. It is not stored or transmitted by Flâneur itself. Your coordinates are sent to the third-party services listed below only as needed to fetch nearby information (for example, map tiles, nearby places, or local weather) — and only while you use those features. You can revoke location access at any time in your browser or device settings.

Third-party services

Flâneur calls a small number of third-party services to provide maps and live data. When it does, those providers may receive your approximate location (as coordinates) or a search query, and their own privacy policies govern that data. Flâneur does not control these services and does not receive a copy of what you send them.

What we don’t do

  • No user accounts and no passwords.
  • No advertising and no ad networks.
  • No behavioural or cross-site tracking, and no tracking cookies set by the app. The only analytics is Cloudflare Web Analytics, which is cookieless and reports aggregate page views only — no personal data.
  • No selling or sharing of personal information — there is no personal data collected by us to sell or share.

Children

Flâneur is not directed to children and does not knowingly collect information from children.

Your choices

  • Location: grant or revoke at any time in your browser/device settings.
  • Stored data: clear it by removing this site’s data, or by deleting the installed app.

Changes to this policy

If the app changes in a way that affects this policy — for example, if accounts, payments, advertising or analytics are ever added — this page will be updated and the effective date above revised.

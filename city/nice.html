  • Cascade Dijon, Colline du ChateauVieux Nice

  • Lavoir du MalonatVieux Nice
    The last public wash-house of old Nice, tucked off Rue du Malonat
  • Observatoire de NiceMont Gros
    Hilltop observatory where Garnier built the walls and Eiffel floated the dome
  • Palais de la PrefectureVieux Nice
    Former palace of the Savoy dukes and Sardinian kings, now the prefecture
  • Place GaribaldiCarabacel / Vieux Nice

🏛️ Museums · 5

  • Musee international d'Art naif Anatole JakovskyFabron
    Naive art in a perfumer's chateau, Coty's old villa above the sea
  • Musee MatisseCimiez
    Matisse collection inside a blood-ochre 17th-century villa among the Roman ruins
  • Musee National Marc ChagallCimiez
    Purpose-built home for Chagall's Biblical Message, planned with the painter himself
  • Palais LascarisVieux Nice
    Baroque palace stuffed with old instruments and a 1738 apothecary's shop
  • Villa MassenaPromenade des Anglais
    Belle Epoque palace of a marshal's heir, gardens fronting the sea

  • Cathedrale Orthodoxe Russe Saint-NicolasThiers

  • Cathedrale Sainte-ReparateVieux Nice
    Baroque cathedral on Place Rossetti, named for a martyr who drifted ashore
  • Chapelle de la MisericordeCours Saleya

  • Eglise du GesuVieux Nice
    Jesuit baroque church on Rue Droite that set the template for the Riviera

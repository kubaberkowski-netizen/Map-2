Offbeat & storied places in Antwerp

20 hand-picked offbeat and storied places in Antwerp — Museums, Deep history, Diaspora London, Medieval. A personal, ad-free guide you can sort by distance from where you are. Diamonds, printers and port lore.

🏛️ Museums · 4

  • Maagdenhuis MuseumTheaterbuurt
    A girls' foundling home turned museum, its painted porridge bowls outshining its Rubens.
  • Museum Mayer van den BerghTheaterbuurt
    A collector's pocket palace built to house one mad Bruegel of a woman storming hell.
  • Museum VleeshuisOude Stad
    The butchers' guildhall, bacon-streaked in brick and stone, now stuffed with old instruments.
  • Plantin-Moretus MuseumOude Stad
    A 16th-century printing dynasty's house, presses and library, the only museum on UNESCO's list.

📜 Deep history · 3

  • Antwerpen-CentraalStationsbuurt
    A stone-and-iron 'railway cathedral' so lavish locals nicknamed it the spendthrift palace.
  • Brabo FountainOude Stad
    A bronze hero hurling a severed giant's hand, the gory pun behind the city's name.
  • De RuienOude Stad
    Eight kilometres of medieval canals, vaulted over as open sewers, now walkable below the streets.

🌍 Diaspora London · 2

  • DiamantwijkStationsbuurt
    A few cramped streets where most of the world's rough diamonds still change hands on a handshake.
  • Red Star Line MuseumEilandje
    The dockside sheds where two million emigrants were deloused before sailing to America.

🏰 Medieval · 2

  • Het SteenOude Stad
    The riverside castle gate, oldest building in town, watched over by a leering carved spirit.
  • VlaeykensgangOude Stad
    A 1591 alley of cobblers and the poorest, saved from demolition by an antique dealer.

🗿 Oddities · 2

  • Sint-AnnatunnelSint-Andries
    Working wooden escalators sink you under the Scheldt through a 1933 tiled tube.
  • StadsfeestzaalMeir
    A 1908 festival hall gutted by fire, reborn as a gold-leafed shopping cathedral.

🏘️ Almshouses · 1

  • BegijnhofUniversiteitsbuurt
    A walled village of devout laywomen hidden behind a blind brick wall in the student quarter.

🍺 Historic pubs · 1

  • Cafe Den EngelOude Stad
    A corner bruin cafe on the Grote Markt whose clock sits forever at five to twelve.

🏙️ Art Deco · 1

  • Cogels-OsyleiZurenborg
    An entire boulevard of fin-de-siecle mansions, nearly bulldozed for offices in the 1960s.

🍽️ Institutions · 1

  • De Koninck BreweryBerchem
    The home of the bolleke, brewing the city's amber ale on the old toll boundary since 1833.

🌆 Rooftop views · 1

  • MAS RooftopEilandje
    A free escalator climb through a stacked-warehouse museum to a 360-degree dockland panorama.

🌿 Green & river · 1

  • Rubenshuis GardenOude Stad
    The baroque garden Rubens designed behind his own house, walled off from the busy Wapper.

🛕 Faith & ritual · 1

  • Sint-Pauluskerk Calvary GardenOude Stad
    A theatrical rockery of life-size saints and tormented souls crammed beside a Gothic church.

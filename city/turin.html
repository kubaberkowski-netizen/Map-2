🗿 Oddities · 5

  • Fetta di PolentaVanchiglia
    A nine-floor house just 54 centimetres wide at its thin end.
  • Fontana AngelicaPiazza Solferino
    A four-seasons fountain locals decode as a Masonic map to hidden knowledge.
  • Fontana del Frejus, Piazza StatutoCit Turin
    A railway-tunnel monument that folklore reads as the gate to the underworld.
  • La Pista 500, LingottoNizza Millefonti
    A rooftop car test track turned hanging garden above an old Fiat factory.
  • Portone del DiavoloCentro
    A carved palace door whose serpent knocker folklore blames on the devil.

🏛️ Museums · 5

  • Museo della FruttaSan Salvario
    Eleven hundred uncannily lifelike fake fruits, blemishes and all.
  • Museo EgizioCentro
    The world's oldest Egyptian museum, where Champollion cracked the hieroglyphs.
  • Museo LombrosoSan Salvario
    The discredited skulls and brains of a man who thought crime was written on the face.
  • Museo Nazionale dell'AutomobileNizza Millefonti
    One of the world's oldest car museums, in the city that built Fiat.
  • Pinacoteca dell'Accademia AlbertinaCentro
    Sixty Renaissance cartoons by Gaudenzio Ferrari, unique in the world.

☕ Coffee · 3

  • Caffe Al BicerinPiazza della Consolata
    The 1763 cafe that claims the layered coffee-and-chocolate drink as its own.
  • Caffe Baratti & MilanoGalleria Subalpina
    A gilded 1875 confectioner's tucked inside a glass-roofed arcade.
  • Caffe MulassanoPiazza Castello
    A jewel-box cafe where the Italian tramezzino sandwich was born.

🛕 Faith & ritual · 2

  • Basilica del Corpus DominiCentro
    Built on the spot of Turin's 1453 'miracle of the host'.
  • Museo della SindoneCentro
    The shroud's whole story, told in the crypt beneath its own church.

📜 Deep history · 2

  • Museo Pietro MiccaCittadella
    Down into the 1706 siege tunnels where one sapper blew himself up to save the city.
  • Palazzo MadamaPiazza Castello
    A Roman gate, a medieval castle and a baroque facade fused into one building.

🗄️ Archives · 1

  • Biblioteca RealePiazza Castello
    Under the royal palace, Leonardo's red-chalk self-portrait waits in the dark.

🏰 Medieval · 1

  • Borgo MedievaleParco del Valentino

🌿 Green & river · 6

  • Chinagarten ZürichSeefeld (Zürichhorn)
    An ornate temple-style Chinese garden by the lake, gifted to Zurich by its partner city Kunming as thanks for help with Kunming's water supply.
  • Neuer Botanischer Garten der Universität ZürichWeinegg / Seefeld (Kreis 8)
    Three glass domes on the Zürichberg slope shelter tropical, subtropical and savannah climates inside the University of Zurich's botanical garden.
  • Patumbah-ParkRiesbach / Seefeld (Kreis 8)
    The romantic landscaped park around the exotic Villa Patumbah, a Sumatra-tobacco fortune's mansion, now a free public garden in Seefeld.
  • RechberggartenAltstadt / Hochschulen (Kreis 1)
    A perfectly preserved baroque terraced garden hidden behind a patrician palais on the Hirschengraben, open to the public above the old town.
  • Sukkulenten-Sammlung ZürichEnge (Mythenquai)
    A free greenhouse collection of thousands of cacti and succulents near the lakeshore at Mythenquai, one of the world's largest of its kind.
  • WerdinselHöngg / Au (Kreis 10)
    A wild little island in the Limmat where Zurich comes to swim, sunbathe and forage wild garlic in spring, ringed by a free public river pool.

  • Friedhof SihlfeldSihlfeld (Kreis 3)
